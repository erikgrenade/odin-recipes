Pizza Recipe

Description

There's nothing we love more than a great artisan pizza, with the perfect supple charred crust and gooey toppings. But of course, there’s not time for that every day. Here’s a great shortcut to pizza night: Pita Pizzas!

This recipe uses pita bread to stand in as the crust of a personal pizza. It's the trick to a quick and easy meal, and it’s especially a hit with kids. Not sure what’s for dinner? Try this recipe: it’s faster than delivery pizza!

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup Homemade Pizza Sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated or shredded Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil
  • Pepperoni
  • Black Pepper
  • Cumin
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Brush both sides of each pita lightly with olive oil.
  3. Top each pita with 3 tablespoons pizza sauce and ¼ cup shredded mozzarella.
  4. Add a sprinkle of grated Parmesan and a small pinch of salt.
  5. Add additional bite-size chopped toppings if desired (but not the basil).
  6. Cook in oven for 5 minutes until crispy
  7. Add Basil to garnish, and serve
