Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Olive Oil
  • Worceister Sauce
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • Garlic Powder
  • Buns
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix the beef, oil, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder salt, and pepper.
  2. Shape the patties.
  3. Preheat a grill or grill pan. Cook burgers until browned on the bottom.
  4. Rest and serve on toasted buns
